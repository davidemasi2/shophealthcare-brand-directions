Sheet B · layers of the system

Five layers between you and coverage. We draw all five.

DWG NOSH-02
01INTAKE
LAYER 01

Your situation, in plain words.

Upload a renewal notice or describe what changed. Nora reads 1095-A, W-2, COBRA letters, and insurance cards and converts them to structured facts.

OUT → SUBSIDY · NETWORK
02SUBSIDY
LAYER 02

APTC + CSR, computed.

Household AGI, FPL percentage, and state-specific CSR tiers are calculated against the current year's premium benchmarks. No estimates — actuals.

IN ← INTAKE · OUT → PLAN INDEX
03NETWORK
LAYER 03

Doctors and Rx, cross-checked.

Every plan in your county is filtered against your doctors, your prescriptions, and your preferred pharmacy. The ones that fail the check are shown, not hidden.

IN ← PLAN INDEX
04HUMAN

Nora never enrolls you alone. A state-licensed agent reviews your recommendation, signs their name, and becomes your contact for every renewal afterward.

We transmit your signed application to HealthCare.gov or your state exchange, track its status, and confirm effective date in writing.

Sheet F · why we exist

The navigator void, and why transparency is the only product.

In late 2025, federal funding for health insurance navigators was reduced sixfold. Millions of Americans — specifically the self-employed and those nearing retirement — lost the trusted advocate that helped them decipher the changing subsidy landscape.

Shophealthcare was built as a response. We treated the brokerage not as a black box to sell through, but as a system to draw — every layer labeled, every handoff visible, every human named. If it happens to your coverage, it appears on a sheet. Nothing hidden.

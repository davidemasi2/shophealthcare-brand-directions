SECTION 03 · WHY WE EXIST

They cut the navigators. We filled the gap.

In late 2025, federal funding for ACA navigators was cut by 90%. Millions of Americans — especially the self-employed and the 55–64 crowd bridging to Medicare — were left to read 40-page plan docs alone.

Shophealthcare is a public-service brokerage: flat fee, licensed humans, no hidden commissions. Every choice shown like a receipt — line by line, in plain English. Not a government site.

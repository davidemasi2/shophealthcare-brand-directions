The navigator void, and why Nora exists.

In late 2025, federal funding for health insurance navigators was reduced sixfold. This left millions of Americans — specifically the self-employed and those nearing retirement — without a trusted advocate to help decipher the changing subsidy landscape.

Nora was built as a response to this void. We believe that technology shouldn't just sell you a plan. It should protect your health and your wallet with the same rigor a concierge broker would — accessible to everyone, instantly.

How it works

Three steps.
No surprises.

We'd rather under-promise and explain the math. Each step takes about a minute, and you can stop at any point without losing what you've entered.

01

Understand your options.

Nora reads the marketplace for your state and your household, and names the three to five plans that genuinely fit. No phantom filters. No dark patterns.

02

Compare the real math.

Premiums, deductibles, networks, drug tiers — shown side by side in plain English. The subsidy math is already built in.

03

Talk to a human, if you'd like.

A licensed, state-credentialed agent can review the final choice with you before you enroll. They're paid the same either way.

What we won't do

We'd rather be honest about the edges than pretend they don't exist.

We don't sell Medicare Advantage.

Different product, different rules, different agents. We'll point you to someone who does it well.

We don't auto-renew without you.

Every renewal gets a fresh read. Insurance changes; so should the comparison.

We don't share your data.

Not with carriers beyond what enrollment requires. Not with marketers. Not ever.

We can't beat the marketplace.

Plans are the same everywhere. We just help you read them.

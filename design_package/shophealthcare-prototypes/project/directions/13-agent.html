FOUNDING CONTEXT

The navigator void, and why we hired 180 people instead.

In late 2025, federal funding for health insurance navigators was reduced sixfold. Millions of Americans — specifically the self-employed and those nearing retirement — lost the trusted advocate that helped them decipher the changing subsidy landscape.

Technology alone won't close that gap. A chatbot can compare plans, but it can't sit on hold with the carrier, file an appeal, or call you back on a Saturday. So we built the AI — and then we hired 180 licensed agents to stand behind it. One human, one name, one signature per plan.

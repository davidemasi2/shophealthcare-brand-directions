A heads-up about Delaware.

In Delaware, this product line is capped at age 60. We can write you a bridge plan that runs through the month of your 61st birthday — and then you’ll need a different option until you reach the 65 transition. We’d rather flag this now than after the paperwork.

What this means in practice: Nora can quote you a bridge today and walk you through the handoff plan when the cap arrives. A licensed broker review is part of the enrollment.

STATE RULE

At 65 you’re in a different product category.

What we sell is private, off-exchange coverage built for under-65 households. After 65 you’re in the Medicare ecosystem, and that’s a different set of products with a different set of brokers and rules. We’d rather hand you the right pointer than pretend our plans fit yours.

Where to look: The federal site at medicare.gov is the canonical starting point, and your state’s SHIP (State Health Insurance Assistance Program) offers free counseling.

09 · FINE PRINT

Mechanics questions.

Still have a question? Contact us — we'll be happy to help.

Is ShopHealthcare a real licensed shop?

Yes. ShopHealthcare is operated by Core Value Insurance Associates, LLC — licensed in all 50 states under NPN #19482230. You can look us up at www.nipr.com before sharing a single piece of personal info.

Is Nora a real person?

Nora is ShopHealthcare’s licensed enrollment assistant — the digital tool through which Core Value Insurance Associates LLC (licensed in all 50 states) does its quoting. She’s available 24/7. We always tell you when you’re talking to her vs. a person.

What’s the catch? How do you make money?

The carrier pays our broker fee — disclosed in the footer, the same place every regulated insurance shop puts it. You pay your monthly premium and nothing else. No upsell, no premium tier, no “concierge” upgrade.

What about my data?

Your card image stays on your device — Nora reads it locally, then discards. We never ask for your SSN. Carriers only see your data when you ask us to enroll. No follow-up spam, no resold leads.

What's the difference between this and a government exchange?

Exchange navigators only show you plans that pay through the exchange. ShopHealthcare is licensed in all 50 states and walks you through every private plan in your state, on or off the exchange, without the upsell or the fee that used to ride on top.

How accurate is the scan?

Nora reads your insurance card, pulls the carrier's published benefit summary, and translates it into plain English. Numbers come straight from the carrier. We always show you the source so you can double-check.

Do you need my email to see the number?

No. The first real quote lands before we’ve collected anything from you. Email is the last thing Nora asks, and only if you’re ready to enroll. Close the tab any time.

Which states are you licensed in?

Core Value Insurance Associates, LLC is licensed in all 50 states. State-specific license numbers (FL, CO, KY, NV, TN resident/non-resident) are listed in the footer.

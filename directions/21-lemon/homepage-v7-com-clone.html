★ FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Still have a question? Contact us — we'll be happy to help.

Is ShopHealthcare a real licensed brokerage?

Yes. ShopHealthcare is operated by Core Value Insurance Associates, LLC — a licensed independent insurance brokerage. NPN #19482230. You can look us up at www.nipr.com before sharing a single piece of personal info.

Is Nora a real person?

No — Nora is our AI insurance agent, trained on a licensed-broker workflow and available 24/7. If you'd rather talk to a person, every Recommended and Premium account has a licensed agent on call. We'll always tell you when you're talking to a human vs. to Nora.

Will you sell or share my data?

No. We never sell or share your info with third-party data brokers. Carriers only see your data when you ask us to enroll you. No "your quote is ready" follow-up spam, no SDR cold-calls, no resold leads.

What's the difference between this and a government exchange?

Exchange navigators only show you plans that pay through the exchange. ShopHealthcare is a licensed brokerage — we walk you through every private plan in your state, on or off the exchange, without the upsell or the fee that used to ride on top.

How accurate is the scan?

Nora reads your insurance card, pulls the carrier's published benefit summary, and translates it into plain English. Numbers come straight from the carrier. We always show you the source so you can double-check.

Do I have to enroll right away?

No. You can scan your card, get the comparison, and close the tab. No email required to see the math — email is the last thing Nora asks, and only when you're ready to enroll. If you don't enroll within 48 hours, the quote expires and we don't follow up.

Which states are you licensed in?

Core Value Insurance Associates, LLC is licensed in all 50 states. State-specific license numbers (FL, CO, KY, NV, TN resident/non-resident) are listed in the footer.

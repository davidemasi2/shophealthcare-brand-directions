The navigator void, and why we exist.

In late 2025, federal funding for health-insurance navigators was reduced sixfold. That left millions of Americans — specifically the self-employed and those in the 55–64 bridge years — without an honest advocate to decipher the off-exchange aisle.

ShopHealthcare was built as a response to that void. We're a licensed brokerage that walks you through every private plan in your state — without the upsell, without the resale, without the fee that used to ride on top.

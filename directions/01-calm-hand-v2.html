The navigator void, and why Nora exists.

In late 2025, federal funding for health insurance navigators was reduced sixfold. This left millions of Americans — specifically the self-employed and those nearing retirement — without a trusted advocate to help them decipher the changing subsidy landscape.

Nora was built as a response to this void. We believe that technology shouldn't just sell you a plan. It should protect your health and your wallet by providing the same level of rigorous analysis that a concierge broker would provide, accessible to everyone instantly.

THE MATH, OPENED UP

No black box. Ever.

Your score is a weighted sum of five factors, each computed from inputs we show you. Every point moves with a reason. Every reason is auditable.

  1. We pull public data.

    Carrier formularies, NAIC complaint data, state denial-rate filings, provider directories. We never buy your health records from a broker.

  2. You tell us what matters.

    Your doctors by name, your prescriptions, your projected 2026 income, your household size. Stored encrypted. Deletable with one click.

  3. We compute the score in public.

    Every factor's weight, formula, and data source is published at /methodology. Nothing is proprietary. Audited annually by Milliman.

  4. You can always export the math.

    Download a PDF with the full calculation — every number, every source, every weight. Share it with a doctor, CPA, or human.

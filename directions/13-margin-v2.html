The navigator void, and why Nora exists.

In late 2025, federal funding for health insurance navigators was cut by 90%. Millions of Americans — the self-employed, the pre-Medicare, the SEP-eligible — lost the trusted advocate who used to read the marketplace for them and run the real-cost math honestly.

Nora was built as a response to that void. Not to sell you a plan. To show you the formula, surface the margin the broker industry prefers to leave buried, and hand you the number — the real one — before you click enroll. The math is the product. Everything else is marketing.

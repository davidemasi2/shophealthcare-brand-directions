Background

The Navigator void — and why Nora exists.

In late 2025, federal funding for health insurance navigators was reduced significantly. This left millions of Americans — specifically the self-employed and those nearing retirement — flood-bound to trusted advocates to help them decipher the changing subsidy landscape.

Nora was built as a response to that void. We believe that technology shouldn't just get you a plan; it should protect your health and your wallet by providing the same level of rigorous analysis that a concierge broker would provide — accessible to everyone instantly.

We are a licensed, independent brokerage. We are not a government site and do not speak for any agency. Our compensation comes from insurers, not from you, and only when you enroll.

SECTION 03 · WHY WE EXIST

They cut the navigators. We built the score.

In late 2025, federal funding for ACA navigators was cut by 90%. Millions of Americans — especially the self-employed and the 55–64 crowd bridging to Medicare — were left to read 40-page plan docs alone.

Shophealthcare is a public-service brokerage, redesigned around a simple idea: every plan should have a score, every score should be explainable, and a licensed human should sign it off. Not a government site.

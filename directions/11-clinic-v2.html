The navigator void, and why Nora exists.

In late 2025, federal funding for health insurance navigators was reduced sixfold. This left millions of Americans — specifically the self-employed and those nearing retirement — without a trusted advocate to decipher the changing subsidy landscape.

Nora was built as a response to this void. Technology shouldn't just sell you a plan. It should protect your health and your wallet by providing the same rigorous analysis a concierge broker would — accessible to everyone, instantly.
